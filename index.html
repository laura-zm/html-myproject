Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce announce engagement

Last August, Travis Kelce proposed to Taylor Swift after they wrapped an episode of his New Heights podcast. The couple kept the engagement secret for two weeks before sharing the news publicly, turning it into one of the most talked-about events of the year.

Taylor Swift buys back her master recordings

Last May, Taylor marked a turning point in her career : she has finally regained control of her recorded music catalog six years after her former label, Big Machine Label Group, sold it to Scooter Braun's Ithaca Holdings without her permission.
